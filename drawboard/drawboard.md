# Block stacking game (temp name)

## Gameplay

- Main concept  
  You play tetris, every line clear deals damage to the mobs on the way. There are 10 levels. Every level is consisted of 2 phases: mobs and a boss fight. Each boss is unique for every floor and is a reference to another classic or popular game.

- Damage calculation

  - Combo table  
    Multiplier
  - Spins  
    All spin+
  - Garbage cancellation  
    +1 damage
  - Back-to-Back cancellation  
    +1 damage if Back-to-Back

- Mechanics

  - SRS+ kick
  - 7 bag system
  - Configurable controls

- Leveling
  - Every level slightly increases the gravity.
  - Every level slightly increases the mob's health.
  - Every level slightly increases the mob's damage.
  - Every level slightly increases the mob's attack speed.

Level 10 would be designed to be impossible, you face the final boss of modern pvp tetris: CZsmall himself.
