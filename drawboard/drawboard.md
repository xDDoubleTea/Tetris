# Block stacking game (temp name)

## Gameplay

- Main concept  
  You play tetris, every line clear deals damage to the mobs on the way. There are 10 levels. Every level is consisted of 2 phases: mobs and a boss. Each boss is unique for every floor and is a reference to another classic or popular game.

- Damage calculation

  - Combo table  
    Multiplier
  - Spins  
    All spin+
  - Garbage cancellation  
    +1 damage
  - Back-to-Back cancellation  
    +1 damage if Back-to-Back

- Mechanics
  - SRS+ kick
  - 7 bag
  - Configurable controls
